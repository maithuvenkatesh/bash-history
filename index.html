BASH HISTORIES

Getting your .bash_history file

  1. Open a command line terminal window.
  2. Run this command: cp ~/.bash_history ~/Desktop. This copies your .bash_history file.
  3. Finally, use this form to upload your script*.
  4. Run: rm ~/Desktop/.bash_history

Please note: this form was created using Google App Script so you may need to give it permission.

Alternate

If you'd prefer, you can also email us your .bash_history files. Just follow the instruction in the Contact Us section.
