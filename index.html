Help us with our project by providing us with your bash history

Why do we want these files?

This data gathering is part of a software engineering research project that I am working on with Earl. We would like to carry out some analysis on your bash_histories to evaluate users' usage of the command line. If you have any more questions then please feel free to contact us - the details are below under "Contact Us".

Instructions

If you cannot view hidden files on your machine then please follow the instructions below to upload your .bash_history file. Otherwise you can directly use this uploader

  • Open a command line terminal window.
  • Run this command to copy your .bash_history file to your Desktop and make it visible.: 
$ cp ~/.bash_history ~/Desktop/bash_history
  • Use this uploader to upload your file*.
  • Run this command to remove it from your Desktop:
$ rm ~/Desktop/.bash_history

*Please note: this uploader was created using Google App Script hence will require your permission.

Alternative Methods

If you prefer not to use the automatic uploader, you can also email us your .bash_history file. Just follow the instruction in the "Contact Us" section, below.
